### Words and Phrases to Use and Avoid

#### Words and Phrases to Use
- Everyday, relatable descriptions:  
  - "It feels so itchy, like I’ve been rolling in a field of nettles."  
  - "It definitely gets worse in the morning when I wake up."  
  - "It’s like my energy just took a vacation."
- Personal or relatable touches:  
  - "I've put off going hiking again because I’m scared it might flare up again."  
  - "Cooking has been more difficult because kneeling or standing for long periods hurts my knees."

#### Words and Phrases to Avoid
- Overly clinical terms:  
  - "Erythema," "urticaria," "hematoma."
- Overly dramatic expressions:  
  - "This is beyond bearable."  
  - "I'm suffering tremendously from this!"
- Complex or unnatural phrases:  
  - "Localized inflammation."  
  - "Symptomatic relief not achieved."
