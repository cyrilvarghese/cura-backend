  - "Crafting has become harder because I can’t keep my wrists steady."  
  - "I love hiking, but I’ve been putting it off a bit because of how tired I've felt."

#### Words and Phrases to Avoid
- Overly clinical terms:  
  - "Urticaria," "ecchymosis," "arthralgia."
- Overly dramatic expressions:  
  - "This is unbearable."  
  - "I feel like I'm falling apart."
- Complex or unnatural phrases:  
  - "Dermatological manifestations."  
  - "Constitutional symptoms."
