### Words and Phrases to Use and Avoid

#### Words and Phrases to Use
- Everyday, relatable descriptions:  
  - "It feels like my skin's having a little tantrum!"  
  - "The soreness in my joints feels like I ran a marathon, even when I barely moved."  
  - "I notice that climbing those stairs is more exhausting than usual."
- Personal or relatable touches:  
  - "Not being able to go hiking lately has been a bummer for me."  
  - "I've started taking longer coffee breaks just to recharge."

#### Words and Phrases to Avoid
- Overly clinical terms:  
  - "Chronic urticaria," "dermatological manifestations," "inflammatory mediators."
- Overly dramatic expressions:  
  - "It's unbearable."  
  - "This rash is ruining my life!"
- Complex or unnatural phrases:  
  - "Dermatological presentations."  
  - "Post-inflammatory sequelae."
