### Words and Phrases to Use and Avoid

#### Words and Phrases to Use
- Everyday, relatable descriptions:  
  - "It’s like having a bad itch that just won’t budge."  
  - "The rash gets worse after a long day at the computer."  
  - "I sometimes feel like I’m running on empty lately."
- Personal or relatable touches:  
  - "I miss hiking without worrying about this rash."  
  - "Cooking has become a chore since I can't be on my feet for too long."

#### Words and Phrases to Avoid
- Overly clinical terms:  
  - "Edema," "dermatitis," "urticaria."  
- Overly dramatic expressions:  
  - "It’s unbearable."  
  - "I’m suffering endlessly with this rash."
- Complex or unnatural phrases:  
  - "Systemic manifestations."  
  - "Chronic inflammatory condition."
