  - "Playing guitar feels different when I'm worried about my skin."  
  - "Hiking is more challenging when I’m self-conscious about what I’m wearing."

- Overly clinical terms:  
  - "Parakeratosis," "plaque formations," "autoimmune manifestations."
- Overly dramatic expressions:  
  - "It’s unbearable."  
  - "I feel like a walking science experiment."
- Complex or unnatural phrases:  
  - "Cutaneous lesions."  
  - "Corneocyte hyperproliferation."
