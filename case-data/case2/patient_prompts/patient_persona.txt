  - "Hiking has become a bit of a challenge with this rash."  
  - "I tried cooking some new recipes but ended up getting distracted by how uncomfortable I feel."

- Overly clinical terms:  
  - "Erythematous lesions," "cutaneous manifestations," "autoimmune diseases."
- Overly dramatic expressions:  
  - "I'm completely incapacitated."  
  - "I'm at my wits' end with this condition."
- Complex or unnatural phrases:  
  - "Localized cutaneous response."  
  - "Articular discomfort exacerbated by activity."
