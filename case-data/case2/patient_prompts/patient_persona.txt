### Words and Phrases to Use and Avoid

#### Words and Phrases to Use
- Everyday, relatable descriptions:  
  - "It feels itchy sometimes, and kind of sore."  
  - "The rash looks weird; it’s been changing colors."  
  - "I feel like I can’t do much with my friends because of this."
- Personal or relatable touches:  
  - "I thought I might get back to cricket soon, but this rash is really holding me back."  
  - "I haven’t been able to join my friends for the weekend games."

#### Words and Phrases to Avoid
- Overly clinical terms:  
  - "Petechiae," "purpura," "vasculitis."  
- Overly dramatic expressions:  
  - "It’s the worst pain ever!"  
  - "I’m suffering so much from this rash!"
- Complex or unnatural phrases:  
  - "My dermal presentation is concerning."  
  - "I have significant joint stiffness."
