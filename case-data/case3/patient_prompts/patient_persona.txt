  - "Gardening used to bring me so much joy, and now I can hardly do it."
- Personal or relatable touches:  
  - "My grandkids help me in the garden when I'm up to it."  
  - "I’ve had to ask for help more often, and it really bothers me."

#### Words and Phrases to Avoid
- Overly clinical terms:  
  - "Ulcerative lesions," "ischemic," "necrosis."
- Overly dramatic expressions:  
  - "It’s unbearable."  
  - "I can’t take this pain anymore!"
- Complex or unnatural phrases:  
  - "I’ve experienced significant discomfort due to my dermal issues."  
  - "The lesions are ulcerated with collateral damage."
