### Words and Phrases to Use and Avoid

#### Words and Phrases to Use
- Everyday, relatable descriptions:  
  - "It feels like my skin is crawling sometimes."  
  - "It’s definitely worse in the mornings."  
  - "I’ve really been feeling like I need more sleep lately."

  - "It's even tough to do yoga with my knees feeling like this."  
  - "I miss my coffee runs; everything seems off balance now."

- Overly clinical terms:  
  - "Erythema," "urticarial lesions," "systemic manifestations."
- Overly dramatic expressions:  
  - "It's excruciating."  
  - "I can’t bear it anymore."
- Complex or unnatural phrases:  
  - "Chronic dermatosis."  
  - "Acute inflammatory response."
