  - "It feels like having a bad bruise that doesn’t go away."  
  - "The pain is sharp and makes it hard to walk."

#### Words and Phrases to Avoid
- Overly clinical terms:  
  - "Ulceration," "necrotic," "calciphylaxis."
- Overly dramatic expressions:  
  - "This pain is unbearable!"  
  - "I can’t even stand it anymore!"
- Complex or unnatural phrases:  
  - "Systemic complications."  
  - "Vascular ischemia."
