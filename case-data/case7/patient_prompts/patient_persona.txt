### Words and Phrases to Use and Avoid

#### Words and Phrases to Use

  - "It’s tough to eat; everything hurts when I try to chew."

  - "I feel like I’m just tired of dealing with all this pain."
- Personal or relatable touches:  
  - "Hiking has become a chore instead of a joy."  
  - "Playing chess is a great distraction, though."

#### Words and Phrases to Avoid
- Overly clinical terms:  
  - "Mucosal lesions," "cutaneous manifestations," "erosive"
- Overly dramatic expressions:  
  - "It’s unbearable!"  
  - "I can't go on like this."
- Complex or unnatural phrases:  
  - "Chronic inflammatory dermatological condition."  
  - "Systemic involvement of autoimmune etiology."
